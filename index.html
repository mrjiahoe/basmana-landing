About Us

BasMana offers city-to-city bus tickets as low as $1 for your travel around the Brunei, all bookable from your smartphone! Our system works – beyond our low price bus tickets and easy travel – great bus service from the moment you book to when you arrive safely and quickly at your destination. The BasMana was designed with the customer in mind, and we can’t wait to hit the road with you!



Our Services

Our Services Range from initial design to deployment anywhere anytime

BasMana is Brunei’s first bus transportation system app made by Karya Digital Sdn Bhd. Initiated by AITI Brunei, the developers team have worked closely with JPD Brunei and local bus operators to collect data and create an application that can fulfill local bus users’ needs.
